Research in the Era of Artificial Intelligence

The Tanta Student Medical Research Conference (TSMRC) 2026 represents a groundbreaking platform where tomorrow's healthcare leaders converge to explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in modern medicine.

This prestigious event brings together medical students, researchers, and industry experts from across Egypt and beyond to share cutting-edge research, engage in meaningful discussions, and shape the future of healthcare delivery.
